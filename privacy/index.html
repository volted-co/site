Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Usage

We collect different types of information depending on the VOLTED offerings you use. This information falls into several categories:

Identity and Access Data

We collect basic account credentials including email addresses and passwords necessary for authentication. For service operation and security purposes, we gather device identifiers, IP addresses, and machine IDs. These identifiers help us maintain secure access to our services and protect against unauthorized use.

Registration Data

When applicable to the service you're using, we may collect personal identification information such as your name and contact details. For business users, we also collect relevant company information. This information helps us provide appropriate service levels and maintain accurate records of service usage. We also store your account preferences to customize your experience.

Service Operation Data

To maintain and improve our services, we collect technical data essential for functionality. This includes configuration settings you've specified, patterns of service usage, and performance metrics. We also gather error and crash reports to help us identify and resolve technical issues, ensuring stable operation of our services.

Analytics and Improvement Data

To enhance our services, we analyze usage patterns and feature interactions. This includes collecting anonymized statistics about how our services are used, tracking error patterns, and gathering performance data. This information helps us understand how our services are being used and guides our development efforts for improvements and new features.

Payment Processing

For purchases and transactions, all payments are processed by Stripe, our trusted payment provider. We do not implement or maintain any payment processing systems ourselves. When making a purchase, you will be redirected to Stripe's secure payment interface where you can safely enter your payment details.

All payment information you provide is handled directly by Stripe, and we only receive transaction confirmation after successful processing. This ensures your payment data remains secure and is handled by a trusted payment processor. For detailed information about how Stripe handles your payment data, you can refer to their privacy policy at https://stripe.com/privacy.

Cookies and online tracking

A cookie is a small file placed on your computer that stores information. Overall, cookies help us provide you with better services. We may use cookies to verify your browser and to maintain your session. These cookies don’t collect personally identifiable information. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. This may prevent you from taking full advantage of the Site. If you opt out of using cookies, we may employ a cookie only to help us remember this preference. If you would like further information on the cookies we employ, please contact us.

Google Analytics is a web analytics service provided by Google. Google Analytics may use cookies or other means to help us analyze how users use VOLTED. The information generated by the cookies about your use of VOLTED (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the Site, compiling reports on Site activity and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google may use the data collected to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google’s behalf. By using this Site, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above. You can also opt out of Google’s advertising tracking cookie or use a browser plugin to opt out of all Google Analytics tracking software. Find more information about Google’s privacy practices here: https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/.
